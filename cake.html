Chocolate Cake

Home

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is a favorite for all chocolate lovers. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add the wet ingredients: water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool before removing from the pan and serving.
