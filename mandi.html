Description

Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish made with rice, meat (usually chicken or lamb), and a blend of aromatic spices. It's known for its smoky flavor and tender meat, often cooked underground in a tandoor-style oven.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or lamb
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Mandi spice mix (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, cumin, coriander)
  • Saffron or turmeric
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate meat with the mandi spices and let sit for a few hours.
  2. Cook the meat until tender, preferably grilled or baked.
  3. Cook rice with onions, tomatoes, and remaining spices.
  4. Layer meat on top of the rice and let it steam together.
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve hot with chutney or salad.
