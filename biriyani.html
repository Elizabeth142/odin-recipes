Description

Biriyani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and either chicken, mutton, or vegetables. It's a celebratory dish enjoyed all over South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Saffron (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook basmati rice until 70% done.
  3. In a pot, layer meat and rice with fried onions and herbs.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 25â€“30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with raita or salad.
